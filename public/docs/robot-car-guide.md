# Robot Car User Guide

## Overview

Welcome to the GoGo Robot Car! This guide will help you get started with assembling, connecting, and controlling your robot car.

---

## Assembly Guide

### Parts Included

- 1x Robot car chassis with motors
- 1x GoGo controller board
- 1x Battery holder
- 4x AA batteries (not included)
- 1x USB cable
- 1x IR remote control

### Assembly Steps

1. **Attach the wheels** - Press each wheel firmly onto the motor shaft until it clicks into place.

2. **Install the batteries** - Insert 4 AA batteries into the battery holder, following the polarity markings (+/-).

3. **Connect the battery holder** - Plug the battery connector into the power port on the GoGo board.

4. **Mount the controller** - Secure the GoGo board onto the chassis using the provided standoffs.

5. **Connect the motors** - Plug the motor cables into ports A and B on the controller board.

---

## Connecting to Your Computer

### USB Connection

1. **Locate the USB port** - Find the micro-USB port on the GoGo controller board.

2. **Connect the cable** - Plug the USB cable into the controller and your computer.

3. **Wait for recognition** - Your computer should detect the device automatically.

4. **Open the web app** - Navigate to the GoGo programming interface in your browser.

5. **Click "Connect"** - Press the connect button and select your GoGo device from the popup.

### Troubleshooting Connection Issues

- Ensure the USB cable supports data transfer (not charge-only)
- Try a different USB port on your computer
- Restart your browser if the device isn't detected
- Check that the controller is powered on

## Using the IR Remote

The robot car can be controlled wirelessly using the included IR remote control. See the [IR Remote Guide](./ir-remote-guide.md) for detailed button functions.

### Basic Remote Operation

1. **Power on** - Turn on the robot car using the power switch.
2. **Point the remote** - Aim the IR remote at the front of the robot.
3. **Press buttons** - Use the directional buttons to control movement.

---

## Programming Your Robot

### Recording Commands

1. Press the **Record** button to start recording
2. Use movement blocks (Forward, Backward, Left, Right) to create your program
3. Press **Stop Recording** when finished

### Playing Back Commands

1. Press the **Play** button to execute your recorded program
2. The robot will perform each command in sequence
3. Press **Stop** at any time to halt execution

## Safety Guidelines

- Always supervise children when using the robot car
- Do not operate near stairs or elevated surfaces
- Keep fingers away from moving wheels
- Remove batteries when not in use for extended periods
- Do not expose to water or moisture
